# Label Definitions

## content_type
- meme: Image or text-based meme format
- headline_commentary: Short commentary reacting to news or events
- screenshot_meme: Screenshots repurposed as memes
- bait_post: Low-effort engagement bait
- spam: Repetitive or promotional low-quality posts

## engagement_quality
- organic_high: Strong genuine interaction
- organic_low: Limited but genuine interaction
- coordinated: Signs of coordinated amplification
- spammy: Artificial or low-quality engagement

## trolling_present
- yes: Intentional provocation or disruption
- no: No clear trolling behavior

## why_it_worked
Short free-text explanation capturing timing, humor, or context.
